Separate baseflow from total flow using a statistical baseflow separation method with two parameters.

Refer to Hughes et al. (2003) Continuous baseflow separation from time series of daily and monthly streamflow data. Water SA, 29 (1).

Parameters
alpha- first parameter for baseflow separation
beta- second parameter for baseflow separation
flow_current- total flow of the current timestep
flow_before- total flow of the previous timestep
sep_flow_before- baseflow of the previous timestep
Returns
baseflow of the current timestep
